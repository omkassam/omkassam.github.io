A little bit about me

Hi! My name is Omar Kassam and I'm a Musician and Filmmaker from the UK!

Music has been a passion of mine from an early age, and I've always loved to sing and play.

I've been playing the Piano since I was six years old and I'm Grade 4 level, soon to start working on Grade 5! I first picked up a guitar at the age of 2, and instantly fell in love! I've owned a guitar pretty much my whole life, but only started taking lessons in 2019. I'm currently working towards Grade 3.

Music Production is something that I've always found interesting. I absolutely love just messing around with all the different sounds, adding effects and making cool sounds!

Filmmaking is another passion of mine. It's amazing what you can do with software! Currently during the Covid lockdown period I have been producing a number of videos for my school, which has really helped to improve my skill! At the time of writing my dream career is to become a film editor at a large movie company like Disney or Illumination.
